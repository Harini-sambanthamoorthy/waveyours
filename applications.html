Microwave Deployment

Terrestrial Links

Crucial for communication in difficult terrains where cable installation is impossible or too expensive.

  • Oceanic Comm: Bridge signals over long sea distances.
  • Terrain Bridges: Connect remote mountain stations without land acquisition.

Satellite Systems

Used globally for broadcasting and mobile communication.

  • Weather Forecasting: High-bandwidth data downlink from orbital sensors.
  • Radio/TV Broadcasting: Reliable wide-area signal distribution.

Infrared Technology

Consumer Electronics

Highly secure, short-range transmission for local device control.

  • Remote Operations: TV remotes and home automation.
  • Local Data Transfer: Peer-to-peer sharing between cell phones in the same room.

Computing & Networking

Secure wireless bridging within closed office spaces.

  • Indoor Links: Preventing cross-room signal eavesdropping.
  • Device Syncing: Synchronizing peripherals with minimal interference.
